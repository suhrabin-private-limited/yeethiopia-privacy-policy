Privacy Policy for YeEthiopia Lijoch SuperApp

Effective Date: July 23, 2025

YeEthiopia Lijoch is an edutainment platform designed to provide engaging, curriculum-aligned learning experiences for children, with parental involvement. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your and your child’s personal information, in compliance with local and international child data protection laws.

Account Creation and Verified Parental Consent

Only parents or legal guardians can create and manage a child’s account. Children are not permitted to sign up independently. During registration, we verify that the account creator is an authorized adult and collect only the minimal information required to personalize the learning experience for the child.

Information We Collect

We collect specific information from both parents (or legal guardians) and children to enhance and personalize the user experience.

From parents or legal guardians, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

From children, we collect:

  • Name or nickname
  • Grade level
  • Gender
  • Learning behavior such as lesson progress and activity choices

We also gather device-related information, such as the model and make of the device, to optimize performance and ensure security.

Importantly, we do not collect sensitive personal identifiers, including:

  • Home address
  • Biometric data
  • Contact information of the child

Third-party Services

YeEthiopia Lijoch does not integrate with any third-party SDKs or external analytics services. All functionality is handled internally by qualified professionals and educators. No user data is shared with external vendors.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to deliver our educational services. Parents or legal guardians may:

  • Request access to stored data
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request full deletion of their child’s account and data

All verified requests will be honored within seven business days.

International Users

Our services are operated from Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, and all data is processed in accordance with applicable local and international child data protection laws. If you access the app from outside Ethiopia, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data as described in this policy.
